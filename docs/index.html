<h2>What this working group produces</h2>

<ul>

<li><strong>Specifications and profiles</strong> for interoperable notice and receipt evidence.</li>

<li><strong>Use cases</strong> that constrain ambiguity and enable conformance testing.</li>

<li><strong>Reference materials</strong> that support implementers and reviewers.</li>

</ul>


<hr>


<h2>Current focus (edit these bullets)</h2>

<ul>

<li>Define the evidence model and minimum artefacts for a durable Notice Receipt.</li>

<li>Clarify how anchoring, event ordering, and receipts support governance and enforcement.</li>

<li>Publish a clear “what is in scope” / “what is out of scope” statement.</li>

</ul>


<p class="small">

Tip: keep the “Current focus” list short and update it before major meetings.

</p>
